Hi!

Virat kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best contemporary batsmen in the world.Kohli captained India Under-19s to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup in Malaysia.

VIDEOS




career

Virat Kohli’s achievements are mind-boggling!His magnificent double centuries in 4 consecutive test matches have beaten Donald Bradman and Rahul Dravid's record. Really a Fantastic feat! A great chase master of India has secured 22 Man of the Match awards in 174 ODIs. His 22 centuries in 101 winning games for India are spectacular. Virat Kohli’s communication with the players creating healthy environment in the team and keeping them motivated are the key factors to make him a phenomenal as a captain. His innovative field settings, excellent decision to unleash pace instead of spin at times and calm demeanor, when wickets were not falling clearly revealed that Kohli has the makings of a captain, who might succeed in all conditions. Virat Kohli is an attacking player but he has molded his style superbly. He is getting better every day and captaincy is getting the best from him. Virat’s positivism is infectious. This books covers some magnificent achievements of a great cricketer of our century!
